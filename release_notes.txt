GhostBoot v1.0 — Per-app bootloader spoof & root hide for Android 10-15.

**Installation:**
1. Flash ghostboot-v1.0.zip in Magisk/KernelSU/APatch
2. Reboot
3. Install ghostboot-companion-debug.apk
4. Open GhostBoot, select banking apps, start service

**Requirements:** Magisk 26.0+ (Zygisk), Android 10-15, arm64-v8a

**What it does:**
- Spoofs bootloader properties (ro.boot.verifiedbootstate → green)
- Hides /data/adb, su binaries, LSPosed files from target apps
- Per-app targeting via companion app
- Zero traces after app closes
